Easy Homemade Horse Treats


Ingredients

  • 3 cups of oatmeal
  • 1/2 cups of flour
  • 2 chopped carrots
  • 1 diced appple
  • 1/4 cups of molasses or honey
  • 1 cup of apple sauce
  • 1 TBSP coconut oil

Directions

  1. Preheat oven to 350'
  2. Grease your cookie sheet
  3. Chop or dice all chopped and diced ingredients
  4. Mix flour and oats in a bowl
  5. Stir in applesauce and molasses or honey
  6. Roll mixture into small balls
  7. Put rolled balls onto the pan and bake for 15 minutes
  8. Use spatula to take them off the pan when done to cool
  9. Let your ponies enjoy!!
